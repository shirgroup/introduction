What is it?

Knotifir is a cloud-based calendar app for planning and sharing schedule with people concerned. While its state of the art scheduling feature enables easier private event organization among friends, users can also discover upcoming public events and import those of their interests.

How it works?

Everyone maintains one or multiple calendar

New created events in calendar can be assigned with tags.

People who followed one calendar gets to see updates and "fork" the event to their own calendars, just like whatâ€™s going on at Github. Users can also push events to and invite other users and smart phone contacts.

In addition to personal use, any other 3rd parties with an updating schedule could also leverage the app to reach out to users, including cinemas, theatres, broadcasting companies and so on. Users could find interested events by public hosts and take part in.

What's more?

Knotifir can be an efficiency improvement tool. Through publish your schedules, friends can be a supervisor for you. Also, we are planning to develop a plug-in that count users' working time every day.

And also, users can search for local events to discover and share interesting things nearby.
